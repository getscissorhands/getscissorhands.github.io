## Services to Publish

You can publish your artifact wherever you want. Here are a few examples but not limited to:

- [GitHub Pages](/docs/publish/github-pages)
- [Netlify](/docs/publish/netlify)
- [Azure Static Web Apps](/docs/publish/azure-static-webapps)
- [Azure Blob Storage](/docs/publish/azure-blob-storage)
